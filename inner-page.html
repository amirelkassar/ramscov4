OUR VISION & MISSION

  1. Home
  2. our vision

In a green economy there is no waste thus our aim is to create “zero waste” eco-villages in the desert land. We hope to transform the Nile delta’s agricultural waste into bio fertilizers & bio energy in order to build sustainable eco-villages. By doing so we will counterbalance overpopulation of greater Cairo and urban areas through rural development and planting previously arid lands.

Ramsco provides cost efficient, innovative and environmentally friendly agriculture technologies through contract farming.

This project was created in order to serve rural communities by creating jobs, pushing for food security and raising the environmental standards for otherwise marginalized members of society.


Our Values

We are committed to creating a brighter future through sustainable development while working tirelessly towards food security, environmental mitigation, and equal gender rights.

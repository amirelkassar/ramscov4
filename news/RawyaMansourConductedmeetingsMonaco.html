    Rawya Mansour Conducted meetings during COP27 with HSH Prince Albert II of Monaco

Rawya Mansour Conducted meetings during COP27 with HSH Prince Albert II of Monaco,which took place in the framework of the reception given by Mrs Rawya Mansour following the 3rd edition of the Ocean Innovators Platform.

HSH Prince Albert II of Monaco had the opportunity to exchange with the Egyptian Prime Minister Dr Mostafa Madbouly, the Minister of Foreign Affairs and President of the COP, Dr Sameh Shoukry as well as with other representatives of the Egyptian government.

In the photo with HSH Prince Albert II of Monaco, Ms Rawya Mansour, the Egyptian Prime Minister Dr Mostafa Madbouly, Mr Mohamed Mansour Mr Olivier Wenden , Miss Hala el Saied The Minister of planning and Economic development and Governer of Sharm El sheikh Dr khaled fouda
